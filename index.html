Nona's

Pizza

Serving Slices of Happiness Since 1960

Welcome to Nona's Pizza, a cherished pizzeria spanning three generations! Founded by Nonna Maria in 1960, our legacy of authentic Italian flavors and secret sauce recipes has made us a community cornerstone. Tony, Nonna Maria's son, added his touch in the '80s, preserving the timeless recipes. Today, under the guidance of her, the third generation brings a modern twist while honoring the authenticity of Nona's. Beyond a place for delectable slices, our pizza shop is a testament to three generations of love and dedication. Join us on this journey through time, taste, and tradition at Nona's Pizza, where each bite tells a story of family, flavor, and the joy of good food.
